My First Post

September 14, 2014 by Michael

I have recently had some big life changes and have decided to start this blog as a personal repository and opportunity to share my learnings.

I just relocated my life from my home town, Edinburgh, to London to take up a new role at SoftLayer, offering an opportunity to deep dive into the growing startup community that London has to offer.

This Blog will cover any topic that takes my particular interest on any given week so look forward to some fascinating insights into the world of startups, technology and the community.

If you would like to get in contact or comment on a blog please reach me via Twitter @michael.donoghu4 or email michael.donoghue1990@gmail.com.

Enjoy.
